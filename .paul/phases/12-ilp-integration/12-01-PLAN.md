<objective>
## Goal
Move ILP optimal extraction logic from `quine-cli/src/main.rs` (currently duplicated in `execute_file_command` and `execute_repl_commands`) into `EngineContext::apply()` in `quine-frontend`, so the CLI no longer imports `quine-solver` or calls `ilp_extract` directly. Delete the now-defunct Phase 12 (Solver Simplification) and Phase 13 (Frontend & CLI Consolidation) from ROADMAP.

## Purpose
Current architecture forces every consumer of `EngineContext` to duplicate ILP extraction plumbing. Moving it into `apply()` centralizes the extraction logic, eliminates the `last_extract_info` hack (where greedy extraction populates `last_extract` but optimal defers to CLI), and removes the `quine-solver` dependency from `quine-cli`.

## Output
- `Atom` and `Term` live in `quine-core` (break `quine-solver → quine-frontend` cycle)
- `quine-solver` no longer depends on `quine-frontend`
- `quine-frontend` depends on `quine-solver`, calls `ilp_extract` inside `apply()`
- `EngineContext.last_extract_info` removed; `last_extract` serves both greedy and optimal
- `quine-cli` loses `quine-solver` dependency; duplicate ILP blocks removed
- ROADMAP Phase 12/13 deleted, replaced with Phase 12 — ILP Integration
</objective>
